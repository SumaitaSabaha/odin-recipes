Chocolate Cake

Description

If you're craving something super chocolatey, this is the perfect recipe for you! This is my moist chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1.25 cups flour
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 0.75 tbsp baking soda
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 0.6 cups vegetable oil
  • 0.75 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 0.75 cups hot water
  • 0.5 tsp coffee
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Add vinegar to milk to make buttermilk and set it aside
  2. Add all the dry ingredients together
  3. In a separate bowl add all the wet ingredients
  4. Make a hot cup of coffee with 0.75 cup of hot water and 0.5 tsp coffee
  5. Add all the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix till you get a smooth batter. Don't over mix
  6. Bake at 320 degrees Farenheit for 30 mins
